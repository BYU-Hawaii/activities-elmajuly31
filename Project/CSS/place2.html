APIA

Apia is the capital of Samoa, a busy but nice place on the coast of Upolu island. It has lively markets, busy streets, and friendly people. You'll see colorful buildings, lots of markets, and old places that show Samoa's history. Whether you're strolling through the lively markets, delving into cultural experiences, or enjoying the natural beauty of the surroundings, Apia has something to offer every traveler.
