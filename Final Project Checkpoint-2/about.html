Our Mission

Welcome to the Guess the Number Game! Our mission is to create an exciting and educational experience for children to enhance their number sense and problem-solving skills. In this game, kids will have fun guessing a secret number within a given range. With each guess, they'll receive feedback to help them get closer to the correct answer. Let's start guessing and see how quickly you can find the hidden number!
